Grinch Punch

Get ready to add a festive twist to your holiday celebrations with our whimsical Grinch Punch! Inspired by the beloved Dr. Seuss character, this vibrant green beverage is a fun and flavorful addition to your Christmas gatherings. With a delightful combination of fruity flavors and a sparkling fizz, it's sure to bring a smile to the faces of both kids and adults alike.

Our Grinch Punch is a delightful mix of lime sherbet, pineapple juice, lemon-lime soda, and a dash of pineapple or lime sorbet for that extra burst of color and taste. The result is a refreshingly tangy and sweet concoction that perfectly captures the holiday spirit. As you serve this cheerful drink in glasses or a punch bowl, the bright green hue and fizzy bubbles create a spectacle that mirrors the Grinch's heart-growing moment in Whoville.

Whether you're hosting a holiday party, a cozy family movie night, or just want to add a dash of whimsy to your Christmas festivities, the Grinch Punch is a must-try. It's not only delicious but also a visual delight that will have your guests toasting to the holiday season in style. Get ready to raise your glass and toast to the Grinch with this festive, easy-to-make punch that's sure to become a holiday tradition in your home!

Ingredients

  • 2 quarts lime sherbet, sitting at room temperature for a few minutes to soften
  • 1 (1 liter) bottle chilled lemon-lime soda (such as Sprite®)
  • 8 cups (1/2 gallon) chilled Hawaiian Punch® Green Berry Rush® juice drink
  • 2 cups chilled pineapple juice, plus more for coating glass rims
  • 1 glass red sanding sugar, for coating glass rims

Steps

  1. Scoop softened lime sherbet into the bottom of a large punch bowl.
  2. Pour the lemon-lime soda, Hawaiian Punch, and 2 cups pineapple juice over the sherbet and stir.
  3. Place red sanding sugar in a shallow dish, and some additional pineapple juice in a separate shallow dish. Dip the rims of your serving glasses in the pineapple juice, then in the sanding sugar to coat the rims generously.
  4. Ladle the punch into the prepared glasses.
